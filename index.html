Message From Principal

Welcome to St. Tara Academy


It’s a privilege to serve as the principal of St. Tara Academy, Miyanwala, Dehradun. You have joined an extraordinary community dedicated towards learning each day, we have an outstanding team of Teachers to make your child’s learning engaging and fun. In our website you will find answers to basic questions about the school and the academic process. We have an approach that inculcates various qualities in your child, such as character building, self-confidence, moral values.

After mother it is the teacher who takes upon the responsibility of the child and puts in all his/her efforts to develop the mental, physical, social, intellectual and spiritual awareness in the child and guides the child to practice and apply these principles throughout his/her life. Through the Schools and parents constant active participation we together can nurture your child to grow to the highest potential, not only Success in Academics and Sports, but a connection to the world and a sense of Social Responsibility.

Your Trust in us is our biggest strength

With Thanks and Regards

Mrs. MohiniDhyani

Principal
